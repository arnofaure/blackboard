BLACKBOARD

A simple drawing board

Very easy to use:

  • Choose your color
  • Hold down the left mouse button (or your finger) to draw
  • Erase with "O"
  • Clean the board with "X"

Save you artwork: right-click (or crtl+click) anywhere on the canvas and select "Save image as..."
